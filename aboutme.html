Meet the Developer

Jekko Syquia graduated at the George Washington University with a bachelor of science in computer science. He is enthusiastic about handheld devices, augmented and virtual reality development, and computer graphics.
Also passionate about graphics, Jekko was a winner at the Adobe Creative Jam of 2018 for graphics. During his time at GW, Jekko worked as a senior laboratory technician and web developer at the School of Engineering and Applied Sciences. He has worked closely with the faculty to facilitate the judging of the Research and Development showcase and Festival of Animation.
Recently, he was awarded The Gary and Judy Bard Entrepreneurial Engineering Endowment Award at the George Washington University recognizing his Android application PhotoComposer. Jekko is looking forward to entering mobile/software/VR/AR development as a professional career. Jekko is from Waldorf, Maryland.
